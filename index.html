Location

Coed Hills Rural Artspace is located two miles east of Cowbridge in St Hilary just off the A48. Below you can find a map and directions showing how to get there from Culverhouse Cross, along with the address and contact details.

Directions

  • From Culverhouse Cross, head West on the A48 towards Cowbridge for 12 Miles
  • Take a left on Llantrithyd Road, sign posted, St Hilary
  • Follow the road down and from here there will be signs directing you to the site.
